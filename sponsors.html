Sponsorship Benefits

As a sponsor of Delta Sigma Pi, we will serve as your liaison to the University of California, Davis student body. Our organization has had twenty years of experience hosting high impact, high visibility events for the UC Davis community, and our marketing efforts consistently reach an audience of over 3,000+ UC Davis students, faculty and staff. Sponsorship benefits include having your company name and logo displayed at events and on multiple social media platforms. Our sponsorship levels outline flexible guidelines that are customizable to the particular goals of each sponsor. Through a partnership, we hope to establish a mutually beneficial relationship that enables our organization to continue providing high quality events for both the campus and the community.

Isabel Ma | ima@ucdavis.edu
Vice President of Public Relations | Delta Sigma Pi - Nu Rho Chapter
