Presidential Statement

At Delta Sigma Pi, we pride ourselves on being more than America’s premier business Fraternity that commits itself to service, brotherhood, and business practices. The Nu Rho Chapter at UC Davis devotes itself to the greater ideal of undertaking moments of reinvention in order to push its members and community to be the best versions of themselves. Our composition is built strongly upon the foundation of growth-orientation, as a way to achieve sustainable results and practices. The alumni who have built upon this foundation have continued on as business leaders in the varied fields of consulting, finance, entrepreneurship, and more. Through the combination of these two forces, we have built an ideal platform of growth for those who are driven, passionate, willing, and humble. We seek people who are not only excited at the notion of learning, but also capable of teaching and reinventing the chapter in the best ways possible. Nu Rho has seen twenty-one years of excellence through these kinds of people. We hope these are your kinds of people as well.

Sincerely,

Jin Zhang | jnfzhang@ucdavis.edu
