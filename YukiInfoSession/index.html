Why Info Sessions?

Information Sessions are a great way for students to learn about an organization and potential opportunities, as well as network at the same time. Delta Sigma Pi is providing you with the unique opportunity to connect with recruiters and get your foot in the door, setting you up for your future.

Delta Sigma Pi

Delta Sigma Pi is a professional fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities. To promote scholarship, social activity and the association of students for their mutual advancement by research and practice.
