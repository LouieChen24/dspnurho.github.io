WHY JOIN DELTA SIGMA PI?

One of the advantages of joining Delta Sigma Pi is having the chance to participate in the pledging program. This program offers individuals the unique opportunity to operate in an environment that promotes rapid growth and encourages exploration into a variety of skill sets. In addition to curating your interviewing skills, event-planning experience, and public speaking skills, you have the chance to network across a wide range of individuals, ranging from fellow students to alumni to business owners. Through pledging, you will learn and grow alongside your pledge brothers, and have access to a support system that will push you to constantly learn and take on new things. The relationships you create, and the professional skills you gain, will carry forward throughout your collegiate experience and beyond.

WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT FROM US

Case Competitions

Each year, we partner with companies like Deloitte, Equilar, Target, VSP, and AT&T to bring our trademark Case Competitions to UC Davis. All teams have a chance to win internships and cash prizes with these companies.

Davis Consulting Group

Our brothers run, Davis Consulting Group, a student-run consulting organization, to provide high quality, pro-bono advisory services to help companies and organizations reach their business needs.

Company Relations

We have toured various companies including Pinterest, Blackrock, LinkedIn, and Tesla and have held private company information sesions with businesses like Accenture, Deloitte, Target, AT&T, and Equilar.

Alumni Mentorship

We take pride in our 21 years of Delta Sigma Pi alumni who return to UC Davis each year to share their stories, give career advice, and mentor with current brothers, and stayinvolved with our Chapter.

FAQ

Q: How can a business fraternity enhance my college experience?

A: You should join a business fraternity in order to make your college career professionally worthwhile. While it is possible to obtain various intenships and professional knowledge without the aid of a professional business fraternity, becoming a brother opens the door to our vast alumni network in various industries, professional events, recruitment events to companies such as Deloitte, and community service events to further enrich the college experience. Brotherhood is something that cannot be mimicked, and is one of the most important aspects of the fraternity. Once you join Delta Sigma Pi, you are a brother for life.

Q: What is the pledging program?

A: The pledge program is designed to build brotherhood and professionalism and prepare each pledge to become a brother in the fraternity. Pledges receive an opportunity to gain exposure to various aspects of the business world through workshops, presentations, interview practice, and more. Its time commitment is equivalent to a part-time internship and lasts roughly around eight weeks.
